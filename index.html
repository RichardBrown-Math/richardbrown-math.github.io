Abstract

I am a professor of mathematics with research interests in mathematical physics, graph theory, and algebra. My work focuses on exploring the interplay between abstract mathematical structures and their applications in physical systems, with a particular emphasis on symmetry, networks, and combinatorial properties. I am passionate about advancing mathematical theory while fostering interdisciplinary collaboration and mentoring students in their academic and research journeys.
